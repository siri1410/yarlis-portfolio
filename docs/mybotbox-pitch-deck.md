### AI Token Usage (Pay-as-you-go)

- Pass-through AI costs + **20-30% margin**
- Transparent pricing (industry standard)
- Users see exactly what they pay for

### Why Tokens > Runs/min

| Runs/min (Old) | Tokens (Recommended) |
|----------------|----------------------|
| Artificial limits | Natural usage |
| Confusing | Familiar (OpenAI trained users) |
| Fixed cost | Fair cost |
| Low margin | High margin |
