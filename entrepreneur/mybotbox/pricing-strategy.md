# MyBotBox Pricing Strategy

> Version: 1.0.0 | Last Updated: 2026-02-16

---

## 📊 Pricing Model: Hybrid

**Subscription + Usage-Based AI Tokens**

### Why Hybrid?

| Model | Pros | Cons |
|-------|------|------|
| Flat subscription | Predictable | Leaves money on table |
| Pure usage | Fair | Unpredictable for users |
| **Hybrid** | Best of both | Slightly complex |

## 🎯 AI Token Pricing

### Pass-Through + Margin

| Provider | Our Cost | Our Price | Margin |
|----------|----------|-----------|--------|
| GPT-4 | $30/1M tokens | $40/1M | 33% |
| GPT-3.5 | $0.50/1M | $0.75/1M | 50% |
| Claude 3 | $15/1M | $20/1M | 33% |

### Display to Users

```
AI Usage This Month
├── GPT-4: 50,000 tokens → $2.00
├── GPT-3.5: 200,000 tokens → $0.15
└── Total: $2.15
```

**Why This Works:**
- Transparent (users understand)
- Fair (pay for what you use)
- Profitable (consistent margin)
